Kith

Relationship Intelligence Platform

Your network,
as a living graph.

Kith reads the messages you get, pulls out the people and the companies behind them, and maps who can actually get you in the door. A multi-agent AI pipeline, a Neo4j relationship graph, and semantic search, in one place.

I

Multimodal extraction

Paste an email or note and a Gemini-powered agent extracts people, companies, titles, and the relationships between them.

II

A real knowledge graph

People and companies become a Neo4j graph. Ask "who can get me into Stripe?" and it walks warm intro paths through your contacts.

III

Semantic search

Vector search over your network with Qdrant and embeddings. Find the right person by meaning, not keywords.

IV

Observability and evals

Every extraction is traced with Langfuse and scored against a fixed eval set, so quality is measured, not guessed.

V

Shipped, not a demo

JWT-secured FastAPI, Postgres, a tested codebase, and a deployed live URL.
